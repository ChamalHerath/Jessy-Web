An Intelligent Travel Assistant!

Travel companion as a mobile application for single travelers who visit Sri Lanka.

Overview

The main objective of implementing the JESSY is to make use of a travel experience, by make travelling a futuristic experience. Given a fact that travelling is an experience gathered in daily activities the objective mentioned above can be achieved easily. However, as pointed out previously. Therefore, the introduction of JESSY has the main objective of finding and recommending solutions to improve the Travelling experiences of people who use conventional travelling applications in their day-to-day life irrespective when, where and what purpose the person uses the application.

01.

Conversational Model

Jessy, the chatbot has developed as an AI-powered chatbot with the potential to comprehend human language and produce responses to the questions directed by the users. Additionally, Jessy has the ability to understand both text and oral commands and to give responses in both ways.

02.

Virtual Tour Guide

The aim of this study was to build a virtual tour guide which could be used by the travelers to get an accurate and reliable information about the places that they are visiting while enhancing their travel experience.

03.

Leisuire Time Planner

The aim of this study was to build a Leisure time and suggesting recommendation to user . In Jessy, we are going to develop some unique features like Identifying nearest tourist visiting places, restaurants, and activities according to the traveler's preferences,. Jessy has the Ability to suggest the best tour plan for user and also it has automated hotel suggesting system.

04.

Tour Guide Suggester

this study proposes a novel intelligent tourism travel agent recommending system, dubbed JESSY, that considered previous feedbacks, social media interactivity mechanisms, as well as real-time data gathered from customers and the tourism board, with the goal of increasing the recommendation rate.

Research Findings

One of the most difficult things for tourists when making travel plans, both before and during their trip, is picking a decent tourist location and selecting a travel agency from the information available. Tourists can select, compare, and make decisions almost immediately thanks to the creation of a recommendation system. The creation of decision models, the ability to forecast user preferences, and the interpretation of the outcomes are all part of this process.

How it works


Screenshot OF JESSY an Intelligent Travel Assistance

The ‘Jessy’: Intelligent Travel Assistance consists of four major components which are a human-centered AI integrated conversational model, an information assistance of the travel places (a virtual tour guide), a feedback and social media data analysis for reliable tourist guide recommendation, and a leisure time planner with a recommendation suggestion system.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this app support multi Language?

Yes, this app support multi language

How do I create tour plan?

Open JESSY mobile app ,then select Leisure time planner >Enter Details and select next > Then system generate nearby travel places based on your preferences ,then you can customized suggested places and press next ,Finally you can see your tour details and direction on the map.

Where can I get JESSY mobile app?

You can get JESSY mobile app in Google play Store ,Apple app Store and also there is link download mobile app in www.sltda.gov.lk

Why should I create an account?

If you have an account, automatically saves any trip you create while logged in. You can then access your trips from other computers and mobile devices.
